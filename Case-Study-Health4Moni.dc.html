The Problem

Health4Moni's online insurance platform had low conversion: customers dropped off during sign-up, KYC verification, and plan comparison. The flow was unclear, jargon-heavy, and lacked trust signals. No one knew where they were in the process.

The Process
1
Stakeholder Interviews & Audit

Ran interviews with product, underwriting, and support teams. Conducted a competitive audit of 5 insurance platforms and heuristic evaluation of the existing flow.

2
Journey Mapping & Personas

Synthesized research into a customer journey map (pain points at each stage) and 3 personas. Identified clarity, trust, and progress tracking as key needs.

3
Iterative Design & Testing

Low-fi wireframes for 9 key screens, then hi-fi prototypes. Ran moderated usability tests with 5 users; reworked sign-in, KYC, and checkout flows based on feedback before developer handoff.

The Outcome
9 Full-featured screens delivered
95% Task completion in testing
Ready For dev handoff
