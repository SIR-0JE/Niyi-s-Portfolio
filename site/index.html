Frequently Asked Questions

What does your design process look like?

+

I start by framing the problem — stakeholder interviews, heuristic and competitive audits — before touching a wireframe. Then personas, journey maps, and user flows ground the design in real needs, and I validate every version with moderated usability testing before handoff.

What tools do you design with?

+

Figma and FigJam for design and collaboration, with Maze and Google Forms for testing and surveys.

Do you only design, or do you also think about the business side?

+

Both — as a startup COO I've owned product and operations end to end, so I design with an eye on the metric a screen is meant to move, not just how it looks.

Are you open to freelance or full-time roles?

+

Yes to both. Reach out on the Contact page and I'll get back to you quickly.
