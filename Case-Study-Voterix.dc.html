The Problem

Campus elections had a 50% voter turnout. Students cited friction points: the ballot flow was long, confusing, and happened during exam season when time was scarce. Every extra step meant more people dropping off.

Process / wireframes image — add your design work here
The Process
1
Research & Interviews

Surveyed 50+ students and ran one-on-one interviews to understand voting barriers. Found time scarcity and ballot complexity were the main friction.

2
Wireframing & Testing

Mapped the current 6-step flow, identified drop-off points, and tested a streamlined 3-step prototype with 8 users. Task completion jumped to 95%.

3
Visual Design & Launch

Built a clean, mobile-first interface emphasizing clarity. Launched for NUAMS election; final turnout: 89%.

The Outcome
89% Voter turnout on launch
39pt Increase from baseline
3 Associations adopted the platform
